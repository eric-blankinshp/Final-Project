Washington Housing Price Evaluation

About This Analysis

This was a housing price analysis on Washington hoising data. For the customer, the team has built a dashboard providing a macro overview of the housing market in Washington . The price allows gives a price visual from a geographic perspective, giving insight on which areas have higher home costs. This aids in making decisions on where to buy property whether it's in a more prominent area or an underdeveloped one. Next you will find a graph plotting crime rate by price, giving an idea of the correlation between the two features. The 'Price by Sqft' tab gives the customer an idea on what square footage is worth in the area. 'Price by Zipcode' is a more more condensed and manageable version of our price map. It allows the customer to see what the average price is for all of our zipcode, aiding in deciding where developments should start for the most profit.
